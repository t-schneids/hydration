Frequently Asked Questions:

How does the hydration tracker measure my water intake?

Our smart bottle is equipped with a chip that tracks the amount of water consumed in real time and syncs with our app to provide insights.

Can I set my own daily water intake goal?

Yes! You can customize your hydration goal based on your personal needs, or you can follow our AI-recommended intake based on factors like age, weight, and activity level.

Does the tracker provide reminders to drink water?

Yes, you can enable notifications to remind you to drink water at specific intervals throughout the day.

Can I track my hydration progress over time?

Absolutely! Our app provides daily, weekly, and monthly progress metrics, showing trends in your water consumption.

How does the tracker help with sustainability?

Every time you refill your bottle, the app tracks how many plastic bottles you've saved, helping you stay aware of your environmental impact.

If I made a mistake logging my water intake, can I edit my hyration progress to fix it?

Yes, you can easily update your water intake in the app to correct any errors or omissions.
